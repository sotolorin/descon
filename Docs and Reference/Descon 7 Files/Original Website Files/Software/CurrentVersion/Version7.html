About Descon Plus software

Omnitech Associates developed and supported Descon software for over 25 years. They made their software the industry standard for steel connection design as they revolutionized the market. Omnitech has decided to pass their software along to a new team who will continue with moving Descon forward.

DesconPlus was founded late in 2012 and took over DesconWin and DesconBrace. We want to focus on renewing customer interest and involvement in this software so that we can develop it in the direction that our customers desire. Since Descon has a very loyal and large customer base, we have no doubt that you will give valuable input.

We hope you will let us give you a better connection design experience, with Descon.

History at Omnitech Associates

Omnitech Associates was founded in 1984 with the idea of developing Descon software for designing steel connections. Omer wanted to save engineers from tedious work of designing connections by hand and increasing their productivity. Descon was first released in DOS version in 1984. Descon Brace was released at the end of 1997 using the Windows OS. At the end of 1997 Descon was upgraded to Windows and the name was changed to DesconWin.
Omer, as a brilliant engineer and programmer, worked meticulously with a lot of discipline and patience to produce Descon. He would enjoy having long talks with Descon users and get feedback from them. The users were his inspiration to always improve and upgrade his work. In essence, our customers were our reviewers and critics.
Omnitech was awarded “Hot Products 2000” Honor award for Descon Brace and “Hot Products 1998” Merit Award for DesconWin by Modern Steel Construction.
(Omer K. Kiciman received his BS and MS in Civil Engineering from Middle East Technical University (METU) in Turkey and received his Ph.D from University of California, Berkeley. He was a professor at METU before moving to the U.S. in 1979.)
